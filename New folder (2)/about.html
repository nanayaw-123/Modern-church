About Our Church

Our Story

Founded in 1980, Grace Church has been serving our community for over 40 years.

We are a vibrant, welcoming community dedicated to spreading God's love and message of hope. Our church family consists of people from all walks of life, united in our faith and commitment to serving others.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, leading people to experience God's love and purpose for their lives.

Our Mission

To love God, love people, and make disciples through worship, fellowship, and service.
